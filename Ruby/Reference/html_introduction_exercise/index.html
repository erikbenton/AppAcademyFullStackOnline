Chicken Teriyaki and Steamed Broccoli

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description:

A simple, tasty, and quick meal for a good amount of protein with healthy veggies. The teriyaki marinade gives the chicken a good amount of tangy saltiness, and the warm rice and broccoli help to pick up the sauce without you feeling too guilty about enjoying it all. Teriyaki is a classic way of cooking many dishes in Japanese/Asian culture, and can be read about on Wikipedia.

Ingredients

  • 0.5lbs Chicken Breast
  • 1 Cup Teriyaki Marinade
  • 1/2 Cup White Rice
  • 2 Cups Frozen Broccoli Florets
    • For Dressing
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Garlic Salt
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Green Onion

Directions

    Prepare the Chicken
  1. Thaw, rinse with cold water, and slice the chicken breast into small (~1") pieces
  2. Place the chicken pieces into a small container and cover with teriyaki marinade
  3. Let the marinading chicken sit covered for 30min - 2hrs, gently mixing every ~30min
    4. Prepare the Rice
  4. Use a rice cooker to quickly prepare your white rice (following cooker's manual)
    5. Prepare the Broccoli
  5. Place the frozen broccoli into a microwaveable safe container
  6. Mostly cover the broccoli with water, ensuring each floret gets at least a little wet
  7. Cook the broccoli in the microwave for 4min @ 100% power
  8. Gently stir the broccoli and microwave for 4min @ 100% power again
  9. Once the broccoli finishes, now is the time to dress up it up with:
    • Shredded Cheddar
    • Garlic Salt
    10. Once the chicken is done marinading, it's time to fry it up!
  10. Warm up a large pan on medium heat with some oil (pick your favorite)
  11. Pour out as much excess marinade from the chicken pieces
  12. Turn up the heat on the pan to Med-Hi and quickly add the chicken
  13. Cook the chicken as evenly as possible on one side for ~2min
    14. *Note - We want to singe/grill the chicken on each side
  14. Flip the chicken over and repeat
  15. Continue until your chicken is cooked through, and dress it up with:
    • Sesame Seeds
    • More teriyaki sauce =P
    16. Bring it all together now =)
  16. Now bring all 3 pieces of the meal together and enjoy!
